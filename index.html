FAQ

Questions students ask before trying

Does it work with handwriting?

It can process clear handwriting, but results vary with writing style and image quality. You can always edit extracted text before generating output.

Can I edit the extracted text?

Yes. After OCR extraction, the text appears in an editable field so you can correct names, formulas, or formatting.

Is this cheating?

ExamSnap is intended for study preparation and practice, not for use during restricted assessments. Follow your school’s academic integrity policy.

What subjects does it support?

It works best with text-based subjects and structured notes. Science and math topics are supported when source text is clear.

Do you store my files?

This demo runs locally in your browser and does not upload files. Production storage policy will be documented before launch.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. The interface is optimized for phone and desktop, so you can quickly generate practice material from either device.
